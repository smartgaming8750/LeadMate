Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SocialSizer Pro

Is this tool really free?

Yes! SocialSizer Pro is 100% free to use with no hidden fees or subscriptions. We believe in providing value to the creator community.

Which platforms are supported?

We support all major social platforms including Instagram (posts, stories, reels), Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, YouTube, Pinterest, TikTok, and more.

Are my images stored on your server?

No, all image processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What are the file size limits?

You can process images up to 10MB each. There's no limit on the number of images you can process, but very large batches may affect browser performance.

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Yes, SocialSizer Pro is fully responsive and works on all modern mobile browsers. However, processing large images on mobile may be slower.
